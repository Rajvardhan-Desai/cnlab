SDP Software Development Project

• Simulates real-world software engineering in teams.

• Integrates design, coding, testing and documentation.

• Builds industry-ready skills and portfolios.

EDI Engineering Design & Innovation

• Encourages problem identification and creative solutions.

• Focus on prototypes, models and proof-of-concept.

• Connects classroom theory with real-life challenges.

DT Design Thinking

• Teaches user-centric approach to problem solving.

• Emphasizes empathy, ideation, prototyping and testing.

• Forms the mindset behind many VIT project courses.

Course Projects

• Embedded inside core and elective subjects.

• Help apply concepts immediately in small implementations.

• Build continuity towards larger SDP / capstone work.
